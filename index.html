PrecisionFix Industries specialises in the manufacturing and bulk supply of industrial-grade metal fasteners — from standard eyelets and washers to complex custom components.

With over a decade of experience serving garment factories, textile units, leather goods producers, and industrial fabricators, we have built a reputation on accurate sizing, durable materials, and dependable delivery.
