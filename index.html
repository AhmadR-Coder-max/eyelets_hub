Product Family · Eyelets

Metal Eyelets — Four Specialisations

Eyelets are our core product and have been since day one. We manufacture them across four distinct application categories — each with its own dimensional standards, material grade, and finish requirements. Every variant is available in multiple sizes and can be produced to your exact specification.

All eyelets are available in brass, stainless steel, coated steel, and custom alloys. Minimum order quantities apply per variant — contact us for details.
01

Shoes Eyelets

Precision-stamped eyelets built for the lacing holes of footwear. Tight tolerances ensure a clean, burr-free finish that won't damage laces or fabric. Used by shoe manufacturers and exporters requiring consistent, high-volume supply.

Brass Nickel Plated Antique Brass Multiple Sizes
02

Leather Eyelets

Heavier-gauge eyelets designed for the stress demands of leather goods — belts, bags, saddlery, and leather garments. The flange width and wall thickness are calibrated to grip leather cleanly without cracking or pull-through under tension.

Brass Stainless Steel Antique Gold Heavy Gauge
03

Garments Eyelets

Lighter, finer eyelets for textile and apparel applications — hoodies, jackets, corsets, and decorative garment detailing. Available in a wide range of finishes to suit brand aesthetics, from matte black to polished gold.

Gunmetal Matte Black Gold Plated Lightweight
04

Tarpaulin & Tent Eyelets

Large-bore, heavy-duty eyelets for tarpaulins, marquee tents, truck covers, and outdoor shelters. Built to handle constant rope tension, weather exposure, and repeated staking without deforming or pulling out of the material.

Zinc Alloy Stainless Steel Anti-Rust Coated Large Bore
05

Tag Eyelets

Small, lightweight eyelets designed for product tags — clothing labels, price tags, luggage tags, and merchandise hangtags. The compact bore and slim barrel allow thin cord or string to pass through cleanly without tearing the tag material.

Brass Nickel Plated Gold Plated Small Bore
06

Custom & Non-Standard Eyelets

If your application doesn't fit a standard size, we manufacture to your drawings. Send us a sample or a spec sheet — we've produced branded eyelets, oversized variants, and special-finish runs for clients across multiple sectors.

Any Size Any Finish OEM Branding Sample First
Product Family · Washers

Washers — Industrial & Eyelet-Paired

We produce washers both as standalone industrial components and as paired backing washers for eyelet sets. Whether you need flat washers for load distribution in a fabrication context, or backing washers to reinforce tarpaulin eyelets, we manufacture them to the correct OD/ID ratio for the application.

Washers can be ordered standalone or as part of an eyelet-and-washer set. All sizes available — we match to your eyelet spec automatically if ordered together.
01

Industrial Flat Washers

Standard flat washers for load distribution in assembly, fabrication, and industrial hardware applications. Available in a full range of OD/ID combinations and thickness grades.

Stainless Steel Mild Steel Aluminum Custom OD/ID
02

Tent & Tarpaulin Washers

Backing washers specifically sized to pair with tent and tarpaulin eyelets. The wider flange spreads the eyelet load across the fabric, preventing tear-out under rope tension and weather stress.

Zinc Coated Stainless Steel Anti-Rust Eyelet-Matched
03

Shoes & Leather Backing Washers

Small-profile washers that sit behind eyelets in footwear and leather goods, reinforcing the fixing point and giving a clean, professional back-face finish. Matched to our shoe and leather eyelet range.

Brass Nickel Small Profile Eyelet-Matched
Product Family · Shoe Hardware

Shoe Hooks — Lace Hooks in Various Types & Sizes

Shoe lace hooks are a more specialised product — and one we've invested in properly. We manufacture speed hooks (D-ring hooks), flat loop hooks, and claw hooks across the standard size range used in boots, dress shoes, and sports footwear. Each type is available in multiple finishes to suit the shoe's aesthetic.

Hooks can be supplied loose or in matched sets with eyelets — useful for boot manufacturers who want a consistent hardware kit from a single supplier.
01

Speed Hooks (D-Ring Hooks)

The most common lacing hook — a D-shaped loop mounted on a flat base plate that crimps onto the boot upper. Fast to lace, secure under tension, and available in the standard size progression for ankle boots and work boots.

Brass Nickel Plated Antique Bronze Sizes 0–5
02

Flat Loop Hooks

A lower-profile hook with a flat oval loop, preferred for dress boots and fashion footwear where a cleaner visual line is needed. Crimps flat against the upper for minimal protrusion.

Gunmetal Gold Plated Matte Black Low Profile
03

Claw Hooks

A stronger hook with a wider throat and claw-grip base plate — used in heavy-duty boots, hiking footwear, and industrial safety shoes where lace tension and pull-out resistance are higher priorities.

Stainless Steel Zinc Alloy Heavy Duty Wide Throat
04

Custom Hook Types

Non-standard hook profiles, custom base plate widths, or proprietary finishes manufactured to your design. We've produced branded hooks for footwear labels who want their hardware to be distinctive.

Custom Profile Any Finish OEM Ready Sample First
Product Family · Sets & Combinations

Eyelet & Washer Sets — Matched & Ready to Use

Many manufacturers find it easier — and more cost-effective — to order eyelet-and-washer sets from a single supplier rather than sourcing components separately and managing sizing compatibility themselves. We offer matched sets for the three most common applications, pre-paired to the correct OD/ID ratios.

Sets are packed and counted together. If you're purchasing both components anyway, ordering as a set reduces lead time and eliminates the risk of mismatched sizing between suppliers.
01

Tent & Tarpaulin Set

Large-bore tent eyelet paired with its matching backing washer. The washer's OD is calculated to distribute the load across the tarpaulin fabric correctly for the eyelet size. Anti-rust coating standard.

Anti-Rust Coated Matched OD/ID 3 Size Options
02

Shoes Eyelet & Washer Set

Shoe eyelet with a matched backing washer — ideal for footwear manufacturers who want clean front and back faces on lacing holes. The washer prevents the eyelet base from cutting into the shoe lining.

Brass / Nickel Paired Sizing 4 Size Options
03

Custom Matched Sets

We'll match any eyelet from our range with the correct washer and supply them as a kit. If you have your own washer spec, we can produce to that instead. Useful for brands standardising their hardware across a product line.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything buyers and factory managers typically ask before placing an order.

What types of metal eyelets does Eyelets Hub manufacture?

We manufacture shoes eyelets, leather eyelets, garments eyelets, and tarpaulin/tent eyelets — all available in brass, stainless steel, and coated alloys in multiple sizes. Custom non-standard eyelets are also produced to client specification.

Where is Eyelets Hub located, and which areas do you deliver to?

We're based at Fateh Garh, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. We supply bulk orders to manufacturers in Lahore, Faisalabad, Sialkot, Karachi, Gujranwala, Multan, and across Pakistan.

What are the minimum order quantities?

MOQs vary by product type. We accept orders from under 1,000 units up to 500,000+ units per run. Contact us for MOQ details on specific products — we work with manufacturers at all scales.

Can you manufacture custom or OEM eyelets to my specification?

Yes. We manufacture custom eyelets and fasteners to your drawings, samples, or written spec. Our process: physical sample for approval first, then bulk production. We handle custom sizes, non-standard profiles, custom finishes, and branded components.

What materials are your eyelets and washers available in?

Brass, nickel-plated steel, stainless steel, zinc alloy, anti-rust coated steel, and custom alloys. For example, tarpaulin eyelets use anti-rust zinc alloy; leather eyelets are typically brass or stainless steel. We recommend the right grade for your application.

How do I get a price quote for bulk eyelets or shoe hooks?

Use the inquiry form above, or call/WhatsApp us directly. Tell us the product, material, size, and quantity. We respond to all quote requests within 24 hours — usually faster during business hours.
